Check-in and Checkout

WhatsApp support is available from Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We offer Self Check-In, allowing arrivals and departures at any time

  • Check-in: From 3:00 PM.
  • Check-out: Until 9:00 AM.

Arrivals before 3:00 PM and departures after 9:00 AM must be communicated to the establishment in advance and will be subject to availability and additional charges. Please check the possibility of a late checkout.

Without prior consultation, if the check-out limit of 9:00 AM is exceeded, half of the contracted accommodation rate will be charged until 2:00 PM. After this time, the full contracted rate will be charged.

House Rules

We take all laws seriously. Remember, discrimination based on race, color, ethnicity, religion, or social origin is a crime.

Please respect other guests by maintaining silence, especially between 10:00 PM and 10:00 AM. Smoking is only permitted outside our premises. A daily cleaning fee of R$100.00 will be charged for rooms with detected smoke odors.

We do not allow visitors in the Hostel premises. Friends, relatives, or colleagues are not allowed without prior authorization via WhatsApp.

Security

It is strictly forbidden:

  • To possess or use drugs within Casa Jardim Studios. If drug use is identified within the hostel, the authorities will be called, and the guest will be asked to leave immediately
  • Smoking inside our premises.
  • Making noise between 10:00 PM and 10:00 AM.
  • Walking in common areas in minimal clothing, sleepwear, or without a shirt
  • Carrying or using materials that may pose a risk to oneself or others.

If the house rules do not meet your needs, you may be asked to find new accommodations within 24 hours, without a refund for the period already used.

Room Cleaning and Housekeeping

Room cleaning and housekeeping are conducted weekly at a time agreed upon between the guest and management via WhatsApp.

Apartment cleaning and housekeeping are carried out daily, from Monday to Friday, between 8:30 AM and 11:30 AM and 1:30 PM to 5:00 PM. We do not offer cleaning services on Sundays, Saturdays, or holidays. If you do not want this service, please inform us via WhatsApp.

To provide the best service to all guests, damaged or heavily soiled bed or bath linens (e.g., shoe marks, blood, etc.) will be charged for or replaced at cost.

We do not clean rooms or replace towels on the day of departure (check-out). If you need this service, it can be arranged separately via WhatsApp.

Lost and Found

Lost items will be kept for a maximum of 30 days from the check-out date. After this period, items will be donated.
